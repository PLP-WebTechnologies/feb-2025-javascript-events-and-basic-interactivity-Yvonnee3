Current Job Opening

Human Resource Assistant

Job Description

We are looking for a detail-oriented and organized Human Resource Assistant to join our team based in Nairobi. The HR Assistant will support the HR department in various administrative tasks, including recruitment, onboarding, employee records management, and benefits administration.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the recruitment process by posting job openings, screening resumes, and scheduling interviews.
  • Support the onboarding process for new hires, including preparing orientation materials and conducting orientation sessions.
  • Maintain employee records and ensure compliance with company policies and legal requirements.
  • Assist in benefits administration, including enrollment, changes, and inquiries.
  • Assist in organizing training and development programs for employees.

Desired Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • 1-2 years of experience in HR or administrative roles.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • Knowledge of HR software and databases is a plus.
